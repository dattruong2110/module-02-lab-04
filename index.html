Terrain Park Lessons

This manual is a guide to teaching beginning terrain park maneuvers to relatively skilled skiers. There is a demand amongst skiers to learn how to use the terrain park and enjoy the thrill of performing. The ski instructor has an opportunity to introduce the students to basic terrain park skills that will help insure a successful outcome in the terrain park. The ski instructor teaching beginning terrain park lessons needs not be a former Olympian capable of performing a “cork 5 tail” or “alley-oop.” Like a football coach who fields a winning team without actually playing, the ski instructor can demonstrate relatively easy basic principles that will assure success in the terrain park.

Adult Lessons

The Snow Centre is open, offering adults the opportunity to learn either with an instructor 1:1 or with friends and family, or join one of our Adult Group Lessons. We offer a range of snowsports activities to enjoy, whether you're prepping for a trip to the mountains or you want to keep your ski legs going closer to home. Whatever your experience, if you're a complete newbie and want to learn to ski, are a season pro or anywhere in between, we've got a lesson to help you learn new skills or perfect old ones. You will be joined by others of the same experience or ability as you, or if you're a beginner you'll be joined by others who've never set foot on the slopes!
